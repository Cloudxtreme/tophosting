How Top Hosting scores each host?

Every hosting passes a regorous tests. There are four factors we take into account.

Speed, we test a web aplication with a high number of concurrency. We test the response time every 100x concurrent users.

Uptime, we have been monitoring every web application making requests every 30 seconds for each web application. Then we measure the uptime trough the month.

Features, each hosting offers additional and unique features that others do not have. These features are very important and are shown on the list.

Support, we like to be in trouble with our web application. We ask a lot. We take into account, response time, number of responses until a solution is found, and although this factor may be subjective, we also take into account kindness.

Price, this may be the most important factor. We update continuously the prices on the list. We search the best price currently available in all hostings.
